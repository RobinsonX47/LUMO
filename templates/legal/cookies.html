Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our service.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be disabled:

  • Session Cookie: Keeps you logged in as you navigate
  • CSRF Token: Protects against security attacks
  • Cookie Consent: Remembers your cookie preferences

2. Functional Cookies (Optional)

These cookies enhance your experience:

  • Remember Me: Keeps you logged in between sessions
  • Language Preference: Remembers your language choice
  • Theme Preference: Saves your display settings

3. Analytics Cookies (Optional)

Help us understand how visitors use our site:

  • Google Analytics: Tracks page views, sessions, and user behavior
  • Performance Monitoring: Identifies slow pages and errors

We use Google Analytics with IP anonymization to protect your privacy.

4. Advertising Cookies (Optional)

Used to show relevant ads:

  • Ad Targeting: Shows ads based on your interests
  • Frequency Control: Limits how often you see an ad
  • Ad Performance: Measures ad effectiveness

We work with third-party ad networks that may use cookies for personalized advertising.

Third-Party Cookies

We use services from third parties that may set their own cookies:

  • Google OAuth: For secure sign-in
  • Google Analytics: For website analytics
  • Ad Networks: For displaying advertisements
  • CDN Providers: For faster content delivery

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

Cookie Duration

Cookie Type Duration
Session Cookies Deleted when you close your browser
Persistent Cookies 30 days (login) or 1 year (preferences)
Analytics Cookies 2 years
Advertising Cookies 90 days to 2 years

Managing Cookies

Cookie Consent

When you first visit our site, you'll see a cookie banner where you can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Accept only essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Note: Blocking essential cookies may prevent you from using certain features.

Opt-Out Links

Opt out of third-party cookies:

  • Google Analytics Opt-Out
  • EU Cookie Opt-Out
  • US Ad Opt-Out

Do Not Track

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature. Currently, there is no industry standard for responding to DNT signals. We do not alter our data collection practices when we detect a DNT signal.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Check this page periodically for changes. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when changes were made.
